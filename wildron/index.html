Marble Blast Wildron

Marble Blast Wildron is a Marble Blast Gold (2003) modification, which development started in 2021. The mod hasn't been publicly known about until December 23rd, 2024. The mod was originally made by the Ajuhku Productions team, comprised of Wildan Z. (Ilmilkia), Zidni (Zen.ID), Aufa (cynprime) and Nugie (grunaku).


Later on on June 19th 2025, pasta_real joined the development team, corporately named PastaReal Games, the production team that made Marble Blast Wonder on its infancy days. Along with plazma, Roblol, bobirika12, Connie (left in 29/12/2025 due to personal projects), Can't Kill Izzy, Gravey and Muffinly, Marble Blast Wildron hopes to bring you a pleasant experience!


The modification is set to have 155 individual levels, ranging from Tutorial to Expert. The demo will only have 38 levels.


With the addition of newly added features such as the cannon, revamped GUIs, new challenge times (Union Time, Wildron Time, V-Time and even a secret one! Shh!), and even new level textures, this modification brings a lively and natural aesthetic.


Playing the mod, you get both the taste of relieveness and infuriance when ascending to the advanded and expert difficulties.
