Marble Blast Super

Marble Blast Super is a Marble Blast modification led by StewMan and Matt P. (AKA HumanGamer). The modification was ahead of its time, with new unique features, levels, graphic designs and music. However, the mod was found out to be stealing source code from the beta of Marble Blast Platinum 1.50 and PlatinumQuest, including multiplayer code. The mod caused a lot of controversy in the Marble Blast forums, and leading to the eventual abandonment of the mod.
